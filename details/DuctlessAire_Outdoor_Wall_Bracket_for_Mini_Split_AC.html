DuctlessAire Outdoor Wall Bracket

Enhance the performance and longevity of your HVAC system with the DuctlessAire Outdoor Wall Bracket. Designed for versatility and strength, this mounting solution is the perfect fit for a wide variety of indoor and outdoor units, including mini splits, window ACs, and heat pumps.

This bracket is engineered with a universal fit to accommodate units ranging from 9000 to 36000 BTU, offering flexibility for both residential and commercial applications. Constructed from high-quality powder-coated hardened steel, it ensures long-term durability and resilience against the elements like rain, sun, and temperature fluctuations.

The built-in vibration control feet help reduce system noise, providing a quieter and more efficient operation. Its elevated design also protects your unit from ground-level moisture and dirt, improving airflow and maintaining optimal performance. Plus, the complete kit makes installation effortless and hassle-free, whether you're working on a single or multi-story home.
