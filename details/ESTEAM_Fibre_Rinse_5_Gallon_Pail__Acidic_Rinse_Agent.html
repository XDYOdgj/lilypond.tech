This product is highly beneficial for maintaining the color vibrancy and integrity of your carpets, especially stain-resistant ones. It helps prevent yellowing and browning, extending the life and appearance of your flooring. Whether you're a professional janitor or a DIY enthusiast, ESTEAM Fibre Rinse is a valuable tool to keep your space looking its best.
