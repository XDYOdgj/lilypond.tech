Durable and Waterproof Door Curtain Panel with Transparent PVC Window

This dual-layer doorway curtain is engineered to provide exceptional comfort and protection. Its high-quality Oxford Cloth exterior ensures durability and moisture resistance, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. The thickened, fluffy silk lining adds an extra layer of warmth and softness, perfect for keeping your space cozy in any season.

Key Features

  • High-quality Oxford Cloth for durability and moisture resistance
  • Double-layered thickened lining for extra warmth and softness
  • Transparent PVC window for clear visibility and safety
  • Easy to install with secure hanging system
  • Windproof and thermal insulated for all seasons

Why Choose This Door Curtain?

Whether you're looking to enhance insulation in a home, restaurant, or commercial space, this curtain is a versatile and practical solution. It effectively blocks cold air in winter and cool air-conditioning leaks in summer while offering soundproofing to create a peaceful environment. Perfect for Any Setting

This product is suitable for use on doors in various environments, including factories, schools, hospitals, and shops.
