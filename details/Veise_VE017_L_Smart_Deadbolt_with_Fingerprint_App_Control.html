The Veise VE017-L Smart Deadbolt – Smart Security for Your Home

Elevate your home's safety with the Veise VE017-L Smart Deadbolt. This cutting-edge lock combines advanced security features with user-friendly access options to make your daily routine safer and more convenient. The FPC fingerprint recognition technology offers remarkable accuracy of 99.99%, allowing you to unlock your door with just a touch. If you prefer a passcode or need to grant access to guests, the deadbolt supports both methods seamlessly.

Stay protected with AES 128-bit encryption that keeps your data secure. Sync your lock with the KK Home app to control access remotely, track who enters your home, and receive instant alerts whenever someone tries to gain entry. Whether you're at work, traveling, or simply away from home, the deadbolt gives you peace of mind with real-time monitoring.

Designed for All Weather and All Situations

Built to endure harsh conditions, the Veise VE017-L Smart Deadbolt is all-weather durable, ensuring reliable performance in extreme temperatures. The matte black finish and dual lever handles provide a sleek, modern look that complements both contemporary and traditional door designs. No wiring required means easy DIY installation, saving you time and hassle during setup.

Keyless Entry for a Smarter Lifestyle

Experience the future of home security with a keyless entry lock that eliminates the need for physical keys. The electronic digital keypad and M1 card support make it simple to manage access for family, friends, or tenants. With a built-in low battery alert and up to 10 months of battery life, you'll never have to worry about sudden lockout or frequent maintenance.

  • Keyless entry with electronic digital keypad and M1 card support
