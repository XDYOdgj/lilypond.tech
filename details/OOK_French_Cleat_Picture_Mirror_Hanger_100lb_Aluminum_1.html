The OOK French Cleat: A Reliable Solution for Hanging Art and Mirrors

Enhance your space with the OOK French Cleat, a strong and stylish hanger designed for secure, level mounting of pictures, mirrors, and art. Constructed from durable aluminum, this hanger combines robust strength with a sleek, modern look that easily complements any interior design. Perfect for homeowners and decorators alike, it ensures your display stays neat and professional without compromising on safety.

With a 100lb weight capacity, the OOK French Cleat is ideal for holding heavier pieces like large mirrors or framed art, making it a great choice for high-traffic areas. No drilling required, this hanger allows for easy installation using the included hardware, saving you time and effort. The interlocking design ensures stability and prevents wobbling, so you can enjoy your artwork with confidence.
