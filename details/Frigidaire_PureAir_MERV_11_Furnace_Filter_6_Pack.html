Frigidaire PureAir MERV 11 Furnace Filter

Transform your home's air quality with the Frigidaire PureAir MERV 11 furnace filter. Designed for superior filtration, this filter is perfect for allergy sufferers and anyone seeking a cleaner, healthier living environment.

Key Features

  • High MERV 11 rating for superior allergen filtration
  • Electrostatic pleated design with dual wire backing
  • Captures common allergens like pollen, dust, and pet hair
  • Easy to install, ensuring clean air in minutes
  • Replace every 3 months for optimal performance

Why Choose This Filter?

With a MERV 11 rating, this filter is built to capture a wide variety of airborne particles, including fine dust, pet dander, and pollen. Its electrostatic design and pleated structure help trap even the smallest particles, improving your indoor air quality without increasing energy costs.

These filters are designed to fit most standard HVAC systems with precise dimensions of 15.81 X 19.81 X 0.81. Replace them every 3 months to maintain optimal performance and keep your air clean and fresh for you and your family.

Note: This filter does not claim to remove or reduce living allergens.

Product Benefits

Enhance your home comfort with a filter that works quietly and efficiently, reducing airborne irritants and supporting better breathing for everyone. The electrostatic pleated technology ensures that your filter runs smoothly, maintaining airflow and reducing strain on your HVAC system. This is a reliable solution for indoor air purification, ideal for households with pets, children, or sensitive individuals.

Frigidaire PureAir MERV 11 furnace filters offer a fresh and clean indoor atmosphere.
