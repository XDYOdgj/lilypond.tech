RELIANCER Magnetic Thermal Insulated Door Curtain

Experience seamless temperature control with the RELIANCER Magnetic Thermal Insulated Door Curtain. This smart door cover is crafted from eco-friendly, ultra-high purity EVA material, providing exceptional insulation to keep your space warm in winter and cool in summer. Its waterproof property ensures durability and protection against external elements, while the magnetic self-closing system offers effortless, hands-free operation, perfect for busy households or those with kids and pets.

The curtain features a stylish translucent design with diamond patterns that allow 70% light transmittance. This not only maintains visibility but also acts as a natural barrier against insects, dust, and debris, enhancing indoor comfort and air quality. Its intelligent design includes both side opening options, as well as a windproof hasp in the middle, keeping the curtain securely in place even during strong winds.

Key Features

  • Waterproof EVA material for superior insulation and long-lasting performance.
  • 50 powerful magnets that ensure automatic and silent closure for convenience and safety.
  • 8 weighted blocks for enhanced wind resistance and stability.
  • 1.6\" widened hook & loop strip for secure and easy installation on various door types.
  • Left/right side opening for flexible access and customization to fit your specific needs.

Whether you're looking for thermal insulation, a windproof solution, or a clean, modern look, this curtain is designed to fit doors up to 32\"W x 80\"H. It's ideal for use on front door, entry door, sliding door, and patio doors, offering a practical yet elegant way to improve your home's energy efficiency and living experience.

With its easy installation and removable strap for side access, the RELIANCER Magnetic Thermal Insulated Door Curtain is a cost-effective, energy-saving solution that enhances indoor comfort year-round. Make your home more inviting, efficient, and protected with this innovative door cover.
