Reliancer Magnetic Thermal Insulated Door Curtain

Transform your living space with the Reliancer Magnetic Thermal Insulated Door Curtain. Designed for all-season comfort, this innovative curtain helps regulate indoor temperatures by keeping warmth in during winter and preventing cold drafts in summer. Ideal for air-conditioned rooms, it's a perfect blend of functionality and style.

Key Features

  • 0.15mm Thick PVC Insulation: Provides superior thermal protection to maintain a pleasant indoor climate throughout the year.
  • 36 Powerful Magnets: Offer a secure, airtight seal for easy and smooth operation, ensuring your space stays comfortable and energy-efficient.
  • Full Frame Magnetic Tape: Designed for effortless installation and a perfect fit on doors up to 70x95 inches.
  • Self-Closing with Gravity Sticks: Keeps the curtain in place even in strong winds, enhancing durability and comfort.

Why Choose This Curtain?

  • Multi-Functional: Perfect for sliding doors, patios, glass doors, and garage doors. Blocks cooking odors, reduces noise, and offers privacy.
  • Weatherproof Design: Ensures your home stays protected from the elements, whether it's a hot summer day or a cold winter night.
  • Energy Efficient: Helps reduce heating and cooling costs by maintaining optimal indoor temperatures and minimizing energy loss.

Ideal For Every Home

From kitchens to bedrooms and beyond, this door curtain is the perfect solution for those looking to enhance their home's comfort, efficiency, and style. Its semi-transparent white finish adds a soft, modern touch while allowing natural light to flow through, making it both practical and aesthetically pleasing.

With a width of 72 inches and height of 96 inches, it's designed to fit most standard doors and offers a versatile, easy-to-use option for your home's entryways. Whether you're adjusting to seasonal changes or simply aiming to create a more inviting indoor environment, this curtain delivers unmatched performance and value.
